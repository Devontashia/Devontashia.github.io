Pumpkin Pie Biscoff Banana Pudding

This cozy fall dessert puts a seasonal twist on classic banana pudding.

Prep Time

45 minutes

Total Time

45 minutes + 6 hours chilling

Yields

12 servings

JUMP TO RECIPE

Ingredients

Biscoff crumbs, pumpkin puree, pudding mix, spices, condensed milk, cream cheese, whipped cream, bananas.

Instructions

Make crust, mix filling, layer everything, chill.

Tips

Tastes better the next day.
