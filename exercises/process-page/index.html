Palette Plate & Gallery

Problem

For this project, I wanted to create a brand identity for “Palette Plate & Gallery.” My main challenge was mixing the palette and plate idea without making the design look confusing.

Solution

I combined both shapes into one logo and used bright colors to make it feel playful and artistic. I sketched a few versions and chose the one that matched the idea the best.

Research & Analysis

I looked at art galleries, studios, and colorful designs to get ideas. I paid attention to how color changes the mood of a design and how playful styles can still look clean. I also tested how different colors would look on a plate without making it too busy.

Ideation

I made four sketches to explore different ways to combine the palette and the plate. I picked the sketch that came closest to the layout I wanted. Then I tried colors from my palette and changed a few until the design looked right.

Iteration

I made small changes as I worked, adjusting the layout and colors until the design felt balanced.

Design Principles

  • Contrast: Bright colors against clean backgrounds.
  • Balance: Mixing playful shapes with simple layout.
  • Hierarchy: Making sure the logo stands out first.
  • Color Theory: Choosing colors that feel creative and energetic.
  • Alignment: Keeping the layout neat and intentional.
