Apple Pie Bread Pudding

This Apple Pie Bread Pudding is the coziest fall dessert for two.It combines warm apple pie filling with soft bread pudding for a sweet treat.

Prep Time: 30 minutes    Cook Time: 20 minutes    Total Time: 1 hour    Yields: 2 to 4 servings

INGREDIENTS

BREAD PUDDING
  • 1 package King's Hawaiian Sweet Rolls, stale
  • 2/3 to 3/4 cup half and half
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup granulated white sugar
  • Pinch of salt
APPLE PIE FILLING
  • 2 large Granny Smith apples, peeled and cubed
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/3 cup light brown sugar
  • 1 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon water

INSTRUCTIONS

MAKE THE APPLE PIE FILLING
  1. Add butter, apples, brown sugar, cinnamon, salt, vanilla, and lemon juice to a pan over medium heat.
  2. Cook for 6-8 minutes until apples soften.
  3. Mix cornstarch and water, then stir into the pan to thicken.
  4. Remove from heat and let cool.
ASSEMBLE AND BAKE THE BREAD PUDDING
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Whisk half and half, melted butter, egg, vanilla, sugar , and salt.
  3. Tear sweet rolls into small pieces and place in a bowl.
  4. Pour custard mixture over bread and gently toss.
  5. Add a layer of soaked bread to two small skillets.
  6. Add a layer of apple filling, then top with more soaked bread.
  7. Bake 15-20 minutes until golden and set.
  8. Serve warm with vanilla ice cream.
