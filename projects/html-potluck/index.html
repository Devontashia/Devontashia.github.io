Honey Muffins

Total Time: 30 min    Makes: 1 dozen    Author: Grammy Liz

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup 2% milk
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1/4 cup honey

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°.
  2. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Mix egg, milk, butter, and honey in another bowl.
  4. Stir wet ingredients into dry ingredients just until moistened.
  5. Fill muffin cups 3/4 full and bake 15-18 minutes.
  6. Cool 5 minutes before removing from pan.


Sugar Cookies

Total Time: 30 min    Makes: 4 dozen    Author: Susie Q

Ingredients

  • 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 ½ cups white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Mix flour, baking soda, and baking powder.
  3. Cream butter and sugar until smooth.
  4. Beat in egg and vanilla.
  5. Gradually blend in flour mixture.
  6. Roll dough into balls and place 2 inches apart.
  7. Bake 8-10 minutes until edges are golden.
